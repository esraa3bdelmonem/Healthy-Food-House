healthy food House

healthy food House is a healthy fast food restaurant, founded in 2022 in Mansoura, Egypt. We believe that you can still eat a meal that is just as tasty as fast food; so, we serve guilt-free food like burgers, pizza, chicken or beef meals, sandwiches, bakery items, and desserts made with all-natural ingredients and low calories. All meals are prepared by nutritionists, providing the correct nutritional value your body needs in every meal. Whether you're on a diet, watching your weight, or just living a healthy life now you can splurge and enjoy the luxury of fast food with healthy food House!
